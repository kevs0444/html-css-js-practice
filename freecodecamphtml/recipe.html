This is the recipe of adobo

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Vinegar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Onion

Instructions

  1. Put onion in hot pan with other Ingredients
  2. Put the chicken in hot water
  3. Wait until the chicken become soft and add soy sauce and vinegar
  4. taste the food if that's you want or add other suplement on it
